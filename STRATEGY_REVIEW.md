## Gaps & Recommendations

### High Priority

1. **Digital Assets Integration**
   - Currently appears as a nav item with no homepage visibility
   - Recommendation: Add a brief section on homepage explaining what digital assets are and who they serve (unclear from website)
   - Or: Remove from nav if not a primary revenue driver

2. **Delivery Framework Detail**
   - Strategy mentions 6-stage framework (Assess → Initiate → Plan → Deliver → Govern → Recover → Transition)
   - Website mentions hybrid governance + Agile but doesn't visualize or explain the stages
   - Recommendation: Add a visual or summary of the framework on `/delivery-framework` or homepage

3. **Risk/Issue/Decision Management**
   - One of six capability cards but links to generic `/pmo-resources` instead of a product
   - Recommendation: Either create a dedicated product, or fold this into an existing pack description

### Medium Priority

4. **Free Tools Depth**
   - Strategy references "checklists" and "governance templates" (plural)
   - Only one checklist visible on homepage
   - Recommendation: Audit `/free-tools` page and ensure all available free resources are discoverable

5. **Templates Navigation**
   - Strategy: standalone "Templates" nav item
   - Actual: "Templates" nested under Resources, links to product anchors
   - Recommendation: Consider `/templates` as a category page listing all templates, or clarify that "Templates" = the four product packs

6. **Consulting Service Detail**
   - Consulting page exists but breadth of services isn't clear from homepage
   - Recommendation: Homepage consulting section could expand slightly to highlight 2-3 key service areas

### Low Priority

7. **Trust Indicators**
   - Website displays: "Senior delivery consulting | PMO templates and free tools | Hybrid delivery framework | Australian company"
   - Recommendation: Consider adding credibility signals (e.g., years of experience, clients served, testimonials, certifications) if available
