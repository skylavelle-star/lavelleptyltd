Lavelle Pty Ltd is the commercial operating company for:
1. PMO resources and templates
2. Project delivery frameworks
3. Free tools and checklists
4. Paid product packs
5. Consulting and contract project management
6. Digital asset development

It is the revenue engine of the ecosystem.

## Portfolio Monetisation Priority

This site operates within a five-tier portfolio ordered by expected revenue per hour over 12 months.

**TIER 1 - Lavelle Pty Ltd: Revenue Engine**
Highest expected revenue per hour. Buyers exist; inventory does not. Streams: (1) PMO product packs - ship Steering Committee Pack at $147 first, then Project Recovery, Business Case, Procurement; bundle four-pack at $797-$997. (2) Enterprise licensing $3,000-$8,000/year multi-seat to government, universities and consulting firms. (3) Consulting and contract project management with Sky Lavelle as principal. (4) Lead generation via free Project Recovery Checklist. (5) Digital asset portfolio.

**TIER 2 - Queenslander Built**
Strongest commercial asset outside Lavelle. $200k-$500k transaction values; $331,620 Coorparoo cost data is the moat. Streams ranked by speed of unlock: (1) Trade lead generation - $75-$150/lead or 3-7% referral on accepted contracts, route to 2-3 partner builders/raisers/engineers per region. One referred build covers a year. (2) Premium partner directory tiers $1,200-$2,400/year. (3) Paid digital products - Budget Toolkit ($39-$79), Project Control Pack ($79-$149), Readiness Pack ($49-$99), Owner-Builder Manual ($79-$149), QBCC Course Prep ($49), Council DA Toolkit ($99). Note: site currently shows Budget Tracker free - close this gap. (4) Email list monetisation via Beehiiv.

**TIER 3 - Darby Street**
Hyperlocal directory with a finite, knowable customer list (35+ businesses). Tractable direct sales. Replication is the multiplier. Streams: (1) Tiered local advertising - free basic / $40-$80/mo enhanced / $200-$400/mo featured; 25% conversion on enhanced returns $12-$24k/year. (2) Newsletter and event sponsorship - $50-$150/event, $100-$300/issue. Wire newsletter integration. (3) Sponsored guides and seasonal campaigns. (4) Affiliate bookings. (5) Replication to beaumontstreet, hunterstreet, kingstreetnewcastle.

**TIER 4 - Newcastle Daycare**
Single-city ceiling is moderate; replication is the value play. Trust constraint: no pay-to-rank. Streams: (1) Lead generation per enquiry $15-$30 via tour-request/vacancy forms - protects trust by monetising action, not position. (2) Paid provider listings - Starter $99/mo, Growth $199/mo, Premium $399/mo, all clearly labelled. Requires 75+ listings with verified NQS data first; currently 22. (3) Sponsored ancillary placements - newsletter sponsorships from paediatricians, family lawyers and parenting brands, $150-$500/placement. (4) Family services advertising. (5) Replication to brisbanedaycare, goldcoastdaycare, sydneydaycare.

**TIER 5 - Sky Lavelle: Authority Funnel**
$0 direct revenue by design. Role is converting inbound to Lavelle Pty Ltd engagements. Boundaries: NO products, templates, downloads, pricing or checkout. NO productised consulting offers - all live on Lavelle Pty Ltd. NO downloadable speaking packs. /resources must be thinking/articles index only. Newsletter sponsorships, when viable, sold through Lavelle Pty Ltd's commercial entity. Indirect funnels: inbound consulting enquiries, Project Recovery Brief subscribers, article CTAs, speaking and training requests, trust signals - all routing to Lavelle Pty Ltd.
